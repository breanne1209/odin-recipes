Description

This Lasagna Recipe is easy to make with a creamy ricotta cheese mixture, savory meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese. BONUS: it’s make-ahead and freezer-friendly!

Ingredients

  • 15 oz. ricotta cheese, 2 cups
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, finely diced
  • ¾ lb. ground beef
  • 40 oz. marinara sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2.5 cups mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Combine the cheese filling ingredients in a medium bowl and set aside. Measure out remaining ingredients
  2. Heat olive oil over medium heat and add the diced onions. Let them soften for 10 minutes
  3. Add the ground beef
  4. Add the marinara sauce
  5. Preheat oven to 375°
  6. Begin boiling a large pot of salted pasta water for the lasagna noodles. Once a rapid boil is reached, cook the noodles to al dente according to package instructions
  7. Spread 1 heaping cup of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9 x 13-inch casserole dish
  8. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles, followed by about 1 ½ cups of meat sauce
  9. Bake for 35 minutes
  10. Remove and garnish with fresh parsley
